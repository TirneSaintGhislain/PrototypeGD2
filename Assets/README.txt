Hit the player to evolve your attacks but it will devolve your other attacks. Rock-Paper-Scissors weakness.
CONTROLS
TO Spawn a player press any button .
Then select which type of attack you want for each attack to confirm press West Button.
Then you can spawn another player by repeting the steps above.

TO Move use LeftStick. To Aim (overwrite look at) use RightStick.
TO choose EvolutionType use Arrows Left, Right and Up.
TO Attack use either South button or Right Trigger for Heavy Attack, East Button or Right Shoulder for Light Attack and North button or Left Shoulder for Dash Attack

If you get stuck in wall you can dash to get out. 

Hit the player and try to get your part of the bar all the way to their side the longest, the most.
Enjoy!
